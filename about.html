The Mount Machi Journey

Mount Machi Resort began as a vision to create a sanctuary where people could escape the chaos of urban life and reconnect with nature. What started as a small family-run retreat has blossomed into Maharashtra's premier eco-friendly resort.

Our founder, inspired by childhood memories of family vacations in the Western Ghats, envisioned a space where modern comfort meets untouched natural beauty. After years of careful planning and sustainable development, Mount Machi opened its doors, quickly becoming the go-to destination for those seeking peace, adventure, and authentic connection.

"We don't just host guests; we create custodians of nature who leave with a renewed appreciation for our planet."

Today, Mount Machi stands as a testament to sustainable tourism, balancing luxury with environmental responsibility. Every tree, every stone, and every smile tells a story of passion, dedication, and love for the natural world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in time depends on your package selection. Standard check-out time is 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available on request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Is advance booking required?

Yes, we recommend booking at least 3-7 days in advance, especially for weekends and holidays. Last-minute bookings are subject to availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and bank transfers. A deposit may be required at the time of booking confirmation.

Do you provide transportation services?
